About Us

Established in 2017 in Jalandhar, Osho Designer Tiles is a proprietorship firm specializing in manufacturing and trading premium bathroom products. We offer a wide range of quality items including PVC, Melamine, HDHMR, and Oak Wood PVC Bathroom Vanities, LED Mirrors, Glass Mirrors, Table Top Wash Basins, Handmade Sinks, and Electroplating Sinks. Committed to excellence, we blend craftsmanship with modern designs to meet diverse customer needs.
